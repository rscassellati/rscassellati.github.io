Project Explanation

Created for my Information Visualization class with a group of three other students, this project displays an aggregation of data relating to the United Nation's Sustainable Development goals about renewable energy and electricity access.

Created on a webpage in HTML, CSS, and D3.js, my portion of the project was creating the bubble plot and all associated attributes, like proper sizing of bubbles based on their GDP, coloring bubbles based on what continent they are a part of, as well as many other features. I also worked on linking the view so that it was interactive with the other views (map, bar chart, and pie chart), as well as assisting teammates in creating proper linking to and from the map, handling merge conflicts, and general bug fixes.
